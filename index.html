PlateDetect is a Traffic analytics and access control application developed for Lagos State’s Vehicle Inspection Service to track, monitor and book traffic offenders.

LASG VIS’s PlateDetect ensures that all vehicle documentation (vehicle license, Insurance policy, Road worthiness certificate, Driver’s license, Hackney permit (Commercial vehicles only), Lagos State Drivers’ Institute card ( Commercial vehicles) can be verified and tickets raised for violators.

How to pay your Bill
  • Please Present your vehicle plate number in the "Plate Number" or,
  • Present your Bill Number in the "Bill Number" field
  • Confirm the transaction and pay with your ATM Card Bank Account or, USSD
  • Note your payment reference received to reprint your Payment confirmation
How to contest Your bill
  • Please search for your bill using either your Vehicle Plate Number or Bill Number
  • Click on the "Contest Bill" button
  • Upload your evidence of valid documents to seek redress. If your application is denied, ensure payment is made within Seven (7) days to avoid further sanctions
